$r$, radius
(h, k) center point
$$
(x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2
$$

- if point $(x,y)$ is **ON** the circle $\rightarrow x^2 + y^2 = r^2$
- if point $(x,y)$ is **OUTSIDE** the circle $\rightarrow x^2 + y^2 \gt r^2$
- if point $(x,y)$ is **INSIDE** the circle $\rightarrow x^2 + y^2 \lt r^2$
